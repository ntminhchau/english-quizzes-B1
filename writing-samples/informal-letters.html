8. Vô ý làm hỏng đồ

You recently stayed at your friend’s place to look after the house while he/she is away. You accidentally damaged something in his/her house. Write a letter to your friend. In your letter, you should:

  • describe what the accident was
  • explain when it happened
  • suggest how the damage can be fixed.

Dear Sarah,


I hope you're having a wonderful time in Paris. I'm writing to apologise for something that happened while I was looking after your house. I feel absolutely terrible about it.


First of all, I am so sorry. There was an accident and I'm afraid I've damaged your motorbike. It happened last night at around 9 PM. I had taken it out to get some dinner in the city centre. On the way back, it suddenly started to rain very heavily. I tried to get home quickly, but the road was slippery and it was difficult to see. I lost control for a moment, and the bike slipped and fell.


Thankfully, I'm completely fine and wasn't hurt, but one of the plastic side panels on the motorbike cracked when it hit the ground.


I sincerely apologize again for my carelessness. I have already taken the bike to a repair shop and had the panel replaced, and I've paid for all the costs. It looks as good as new now, but if you're not satisfied with the repair when you get back, please let me know. We can figure something else out, and I'm of course happy to cover any further costs.


Hope to hear from you soon.


All the best,

Lai
